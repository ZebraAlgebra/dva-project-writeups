\subsection*{Project Content}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Topic}]{Interesting, relevant to course, of suitable difficulty. Justify it.}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Dataset}]{Large and Real, e.g. couple hundreds of thousand of rows, couple hunderd MBs.}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Analysis}]{Perform non-trivial algorithm/analysis/computation.}

\subsection*{Heilmeier}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q1}]{What are you trying to do? Articulate objectives, no jargon.}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q2}]{How is it done today; what are the limits of current practice?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q3}]{What’s new in your approach? Why will it be successful?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q4}]{Who cares?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q5}]{If you’re successful, what difference and impact will it make, and how do you measure them (e.g., via user studies, experiments, ground truth data, etc.)?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q6}]{What are the risks and payoffs?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q7}]{How much will it cost?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q8}]{How long will it take?}
\todo[caption={Checklist: Heilmeier Q9}]{What are the midterm and final "exams" to check for success? How will progress be measured?}
